# Stack

A sticky note for your brain.

For people who multi-task like crazy — the kind who kick off five background agents, blink, and drift three tasks away from what they meant to do. Stack keeps your async tasks on screen so you don't have to hold them in your head.

<br>

## Features

- **Tracks your async tasks** — everything you've kicked off, in one place
- **Lives on your screen** — one command, and it sticks like a sticky note
- **Mark tasks done** — check things off as you finish
- **Fully local** — your data stays on your machine

<br>

## Usage

```bash
stack
```
